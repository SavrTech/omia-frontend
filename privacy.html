Privacy Policy

Updated: September 11, 2025

At Omia Studio, we are deeply committed to protecting your privacy and maintaining the strictest confidentiality regarding your work. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle, store, and protect your information when you use our platform. We want to assure you that your trust is our highest priority, and we are dedicated to upholding the highest standards of data privacy and security.

1. Project Confidentiality

All project data—including designs, code, assets, and any other information you create or upload to Omia Studio—remains your exclusive property at all times. We do not access, review, analyze, or use your project details for any purpose other than to provide and maintain our services. Your project data will never be used for training AI models, marketing, analytics, or any internal or external research.

2. Data Usage and Sharing

Omia Studio will never sell, share, rent, or disclose your project information to any third party, except as required by law or with your explicit, informed consent. We do not monetize your data in any way. Your intellectual property and creative work are fully protected and remain under your control.

3. Security Measures

We employ industry-leading security practices and technologies to safeguard your data against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. All data transmissions are encrypted, and our infrastructure is regularly monitored and updated to ensure the highest level of protection for your projects.

4. Your Rights and Control

You retain full ownership and control over your projects and data. You may export, modify, or permanently delete your content at any time through the Omia Studio platform. We do not retain any copies of your deleted data.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review this policy periodically.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding your privacy or data security, please contact our support team. We are committed to responding promptly and transparently to all inquiries.

Thank you for trusting Omia Studio with your creative work.
