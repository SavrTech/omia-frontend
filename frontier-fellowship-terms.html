Frontier Fellowship Terms & Conditions

These terms define the standard participation framework for Frontier Fellowship. The program is designed to be supportive, practical, and growth-oriented for serious builders.

Program Package

  • Unlimited app build credits for the active program period.
  • 1000+ AI inference credits for the active program period.
  • Priority support for eligible fellowship participants.
  • Badges and physical swag awarded for reaching program milestones.
  • Completion certification.

Support Experience

Frontier Fellowship is built to help participants ship confidently. You can expect:

  • Regular check-ins with the support team.
  • Community meetups and builder-focused sessions when scheduled.
  • Priority feature access opportunities as they become available.

Billing Policy

  • The program fee is a one-time payment determined by region. It is not recurring or rolling.
  • All fellowship benefits are included in the single program fee. No benefit is billed separately.
  • The Omia Studio team will actively support participants with content, including monetized content opportunities tied to their work in the program.
  • The Omia Studio team will also support participants in freelance work through custom benefits and dedicated technical support.
  • All payments are final and non-refundable.

Eligibility and Participation

  • Participants must provide accurate application details and maintain an active Omia Studio account.
  • Benefits are intended for active project development and may be adjusted if misuse is detected.
  • Omia Studio may update program operations to improve support quality while preserving core commitments.
