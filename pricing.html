Pricing FAQs

Does pricing change when web app support retires?
No. Pricing is unchanged. The web app retirement only shifts the supported experience to desktop.
Can I deploy my project for free?
Free gives you the core editor, collaboration, exports, and previews. Live deployment, AI features, and cloud-based Flutter builds require Pro or higher.
Can I buy more cloud credits?
Yes. You can purchase additional cloud credits separately at any time if your project needs more than the 200 credits included with Pro.
Does the Free tier include any AI support?
No. The Free tier does not include AI support, AI Copilot access, or any included cloud credits.
Do I have to pay for projects?
No. You can create and keep an unlimited number of projects. Your projects are always available to you.
Do you train your AI on my free project's data?
No, we never access user's project data for any reason.
What if I want to build for mobile?
Flutter mobile and desktop builds run on our cloud infrastructure and consume cloud credits. Pro includes 200 cloud credits each month, and you can buy more credits if your project needs additional build capacity.
How is Pro pricing charged?
Pro is charged at 29 USD per project per month and includes 200 cloud credits each month.
